REST EASY

The world may be harsh, the circumstances may require your heaviest efforts,

The world may be stoopid, leaving you eepy-sleepy with constant efforts

The world may be unfair, among the based and red-pilled, you may be dazed and bed-pilled.

But do know, that your efforts are not unseen,

however small or big, they will bring you results.

So rest easy, you little warrior.

Rest easy, but do not give up.

Do not give up.
